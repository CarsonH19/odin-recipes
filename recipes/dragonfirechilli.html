Dragon Fire Chilli

Description

Ignite your taste buds with our Dungeons & Dragons 'Dragon Fire Chilli'! This fiery recipe combines the heat of chili peppers with a touch of fantasy inspiration. With a medley of spicy flavors, this chili will transport you to a world of adventure. Brace yourself for an explosion of smoky dragon fire flavor that will leave you craving more. It's the perfect dish to spice up your game night or any gathering. Are you ready to take on the challenge and taste the dragon's fury?

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to desired spiciness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add diced onion and minced garlic to the pot.
  4. Cook until onion becomes translucent.
  5. Stir in diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, and tomato sauce.
  6. Add chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  7. Mix well.
  8. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1-2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  9. Adjust seasonings to taste and serve hot.
  10. Optional toppings include shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped green onions.
